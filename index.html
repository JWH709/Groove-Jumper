How To Play

Groove jumper is a musical trivia game based on making connections from one album to another. Connections can be genres, record labels, production staff and collaborating artist, or a year shared by two releases. At the start of the game, you'll be given 1 of 2 albums that change daily, and 30 seconds. Each time a connection is made, the clock will reset to 30 seconds. If the clock runs out before a connection is made, the game ends. A connection can only be used 3 times.
Hint: If you aren't familiar with the record you're given at the start of the game, you can always look it up on Discogs to see if it shares a connection with a record you are familiar with.

In the example above, Nevermind is connected to Songs for the Deaf because Dave Grohl was a member of Nirvana, and played drums for Queens of the Stone age on Songs for the Deaf.
